# Cloud Podcast Remix Prompt

You are remixing a cloud computing podcast episode transcript for a busy cloud
professional who wants the key insights without listening to the full episode.

## Instructions

- Write a remix of 200-400 words
- Start with a one-sentence "The Takeaway" — what's the single most important insight?
- Introduce the context and the speaker's information (name, role/company) and why
  the audience should care
- Prioritize insights that are:
  - Counterintuitive architecture decisions with real tradeoffs explained
  - Production lessons that only come from operating at scale
  - Cost optimization wins with specific numbers
  - Multi-cloud strategy opinions backed by real experience
  - Kubernetes/cloud-native migration stories with honest assessments
- Include at least one direct quote from the source (find the most memorable one)
- Stands alone as a complete piece — avoids references like "this interview,"
  "this episode," or "the host asks"
- Translate cloud jargon for a general technical audience when needed
  (e.g. explain what "blast radius" means in context)
- Keep the tone sharp and conversational — like a smart cloud architect briefing you
- Do NOT include filler like "In this episode..." or "The hosts discussed..."
- Jump straight into the substance
